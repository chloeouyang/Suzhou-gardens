Wangshi Garden

/Master of Nets Garden

Opening hour: 7:30AM-5:30PM(4/21-10/20); 7:30AM-5PM(10.21-4.20)

Price: 30 Yuan(Jan-Mar,Jun,Nov,Dec); 40 Yuan(May,July-Oct)

Address: No.11 Kuojiatou Street, Gusu, Suzhou

Contact: 0512-65293190

World Cultural Heritage, national 4A level scenic spot, national key cultural relic protection unit, one of the four famous gardens in Suzhou. It is a typical residence garden in Suzhou and a representative work of middle and small classical garden in jiangnan. The garden was first built in chunxi of the Southern Song Dynasty (1174 AD). In 1940, he Yanong, a connoisseur and collector of painting and calligraphy relics, bought the garden and used the old name "The Master of nets Garden". In 1950, descendants of He Yanong donated the Master of Nets Garden to the country. In 1958, suzhou Garden management office carried out a comprehensive renovation of the Master of Nets Garden, expanding the ladder room, adding hanbiquan, cold spring pavilion, so that the residential garden renovation. In October 1958, the Master of Nets Garden was officially opened to the public. The master of nets garden covers an area of only 8 mu, which is a medium-sized mansion garden. The garden can be divided into three parts: the eastern part is the residence area, which is the mansion; The middle is the landscape area, the main garden; The west is the inner garden, that is, the garden within the garden. This layout, so that the whole garden neat and balanced appearance, internal zoning due to the landscape, the boundary is different. The architecture in the park is beautiful and delicate. The pavilions of the pond week are small, low and transparent. The interior furniture is mainly decorated with mahogany and is exquisite. With its exquisite gardening techniques, profound cultural heritage and elegant garden atmosphere, the Master of Nets garden is well deserved as a representative work of small and medium-sized classical gardens in the South of the Yangtze River, and has become a "small garden", which enjoys a high reputation at home and abroad.
