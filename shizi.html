Shizi Garden
/The Lion Forest Garden

Opening hour: 7:30AM-5:30PM(3/1-10/15); 7:30AM-5PM(10.16-2.29)

Price: 30 Yuan(Jan-Mar,Jun,Nov,Dec); 40 Yuan(May,July-Oct)

Address: No.23 Yuanlin Road, Gusu, Suzhou

Contact: 0512-67773263

Shizilin is one of the four famous gardens in Suzhou. It has a history of more than 600 years and is the representative garden of yuan Dynasty. Located in the northeast corner of suzhou city, Jiangsu Province, the garden road is a rectangle with a slightly wider width from east to west, covering an area of 1.1 hectares and an open area of 0.88 hectares. Shizilin was first built in the Yuan Dynasty. In 1341, the eminent monk Tianru Zen Master Wei Ze came to Suzhou to preach sutras, which was warmly welcomed by his disciples. In the second year of Yuan Zhi Zheng (1342), his disciples bought land and a house to build a zen forest for Tianru Zen master Wei Ze. Because of the park "Suanni Bamboo forest, bamboo under many rocks, like suanni (lion)", and because Tian Such zen master Wei had been good at zhejiang Tianmu Mountain Lion Rock general should be the National teacher Zhongfeng, in order to commemorate the Buddha's clothes and teachings, the relationship between teachers and disciples, take the meaning of Leo in the Buddhist sutra, so named "Shizi Forest", "Lion Forest". Also because of the Buddha book "lion roar" a language (" lion roar "refers to the Buddhist monk to teach scripture), and many rockery like lion shape and named. In 1982, Shizilin was designated as a cultural relic protection unit of Jiangsu Province. Rockery throughout the park, surrounded by the corridor, the tower looming, winding tongyou, there is a general feeling of maze. The walls of the corridor are inlaid with calligraphic stele of su Shi, Mi Fu, Huang Tingjian and CAI Xiang of the Song Dynasty and the stele of Plum Blossom Poem by Wen Tianxiang of the Southern Song Dynasty.
